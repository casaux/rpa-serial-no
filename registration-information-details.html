What is the intended use of your RPA?





You should only register an RPA for business or commercial use if you are the owner of the RPA. If the RPA is owned by an organisation then the RPA should be registered under that organisation.



What is the weight class of your RPA?






If your RPA weighs more than 150kg you are required to register it as a VH Registered Aircraft.
