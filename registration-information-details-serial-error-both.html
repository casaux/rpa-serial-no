How do I find my serial number?
The serial number for your drone is located inside the body of the drone. To find the serial number please remove the battery and you will be able to see the serial number inside the battery compartment. It will be a 14 character serial number split across two lines next to a QR code. You may wish to use a QR code scanner to scan the code and copy and paste this into the drone and RPA registration form.

The serial number also appears on the outside of the box of your Phantom 4. It is under the larger barcode prefixed with "S/N" and is the same 14 character serial you will find inside the battery compartment of your aircraft.

What is the intended use of your drone?





Drones or RPA registered for business or commercial use must be registered by the owner. If the drone or RPA is owned by an organisation, the registration must be in the name of that organisation using their ARN.

If your drone weighs more than 150kg you are required to register it as a VH Registered Aircraft.
